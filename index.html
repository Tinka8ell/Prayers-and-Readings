A prayer of yielding ...

Jesus, I yield myself to You, and to Your ways. I admit that I often count myself out. I feel that my pain is too great, or I feel too broken. I think more about what I can’t do than what I can... In this moment, I offer You the dry, broken and empty parts of my life, and I receive your fullness. I offer You the grief and loss that I feel, and I receive Your comfort. I offer You the smallness, the weakness that I feel, and I chose to receive Your strength.
